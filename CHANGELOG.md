## [1.1.0] - 2026-05-02

### Added
- Added **Enable Redirect** toggle in Project Settings to turn redirection on/off without clearing the startup scene.
- Added **Excluded Scenes** list in Project Settings. Individual scenes or entire directories can be excluded from redirect; scenes inside excluded directories are also excluded recursively.
